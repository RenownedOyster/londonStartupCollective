WHAT WE DO

We are an invitation only start up community of talented makers, coders, & techies motivated by three simple principals:

  1. Money is not the root of all evil, it is a tool
  2. Grinding hard at what you believe in is virtue
  3. A mediocre plan executed now beats the perfect plan executed next month

And more often that not more money does mean a better life. Not only for one's self but for our families, our friends, & the charities we can contribute to.

HOW WE DO IT

We create start ups

We go in hard. While we understand that working long hours is not the life-style for everyone, but it is for us.

We're willing to put in the time it takes to achieve our goals.

We meet up every week as a collective & pool our collective resources to help each other achieve their goals as individuals & as micro think-tanks.
